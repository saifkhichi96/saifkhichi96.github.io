About the Augmented Vision Lab

The Augmented Vision Lab at RPTU, led by Prof. Dr. Didier Stricker, is part of the Intelligent Systems faculty within the Department of Computer Science at RPTU Kaiserslautern. Affiliated with the Augmented Vision Group at DFKI, the lab offers students opportunities to engage in cutting-edge research through seminars, projects, and guided research each semester.

Student Research Initiatives

Since 2023, I have actively supervised various student projects, working closely with students who are eager to explore innovative research topics. If you are a motivated student interested in collaborating on exciting projects, please reach out!
