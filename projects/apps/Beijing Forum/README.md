# Beijing Forum - Islamabad
## Background
[Beijing Forum](http://www.bjf.pku.edu.cn) is an annual academic event hosted by [Peking University](http://english.pku.edu.cn) in Beijing. In 2016, [National University of Sciences and Technology (NUST)](http://www.nust.edu.pk/Events/Pages/Beijing-Forum-2016.aspx) in Islamabad, Pakistan inaugurated the Beijing Forum Overseas Series.

## Objective
The objective of this project was to develop an *Android application* for the *conference attendees* to provide them with *up-to-date information*, *location maps*, and *realtime news* about the conference.

## Overview
### Salient Features
1. Locations related to conference marked on a map (using Google Maps API).
2. News updates received from a backend server in realtime (when application running) and displayed on a notice-board inside the application.
3. Details of the event, including information and schedule of the conference.

### Platforms
- Android v4.0+ ([Download](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nust.beijingforum2k16))

### Timeline
The project was completed on May 13, 2016.

### Team
This project was completed by me in 2016 as a member of Web/IT team in the Organising Committee (OC) of the conference, which was comprised of students of NUST.
