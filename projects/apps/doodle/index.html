Doodle is a complete art tool to help bring out your inner artist.


Discover Doodle!

Express yourself without limits!

With its easy-to-use and intuitive design, Doodle is the perfect addition to your art toolbox.

Powerful Toolbox

Choose between a number of brush sizes and colors, easily draw basic geometric shapes, set fill and stroke colors.

Messenger Expressions

Integrate Doodle with Facebook Messenger to make your conversations richer and more personal.

Distraction-Free

Doodle allows you to focus on your project by providing an option to hide unnecessary controls while you are working.

Share Doodles

Effortlessly share your drawings with friends on social media or send to another device, right from inside the app.

Save Projects

Doodles can be saved and loaded from device memory. You can continue working on a saved project later, or load an base image from Gallery.
