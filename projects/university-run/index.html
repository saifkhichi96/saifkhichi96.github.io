University Run is a 2D infinite runner game, set in NUST, Pakistan. The gameplay consists of a student running away from an evil teacher, who is intent on killing them. The student has to overcome a number of obstacles (in shapes of homeworks, exams, lab equipment, etc.) as he desperately tries to make it to the end of their university degree.
